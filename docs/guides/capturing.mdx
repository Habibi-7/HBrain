## Trigger phrases

The agent captures when you say things like:

- "Remember that…" / "Note that…" / "Save this…"
- "I decided…" / "We decided…" / "I'm going with…"
- "I should…" / "Remind me to…" / "TODO…"
- "Bookmark this…" / "Add to my brain…"

When in doubt, the agent captures silently. A lost thought is worse than a stray event.

## One file per thought

Three thoughts = three files. Don't bundle unrelated captures into one event.

Use `links` frontmatter to connect related events.

## Use your phrasing

The agent captures what you said, not a summary. Your words are the artifact — don't paraphrase.

## File path

```
$BRAIN_DIR/events/YYYY/MM/DD/<ulid>-<slug>.md
```

- Date is the event date in UTC
- ULID encodes the timestamp (time-sortable)
- Slug = 2-5 words from the body, hyphenated

## Confirmation

After writing, the agent confirms briefly:

> "Captured decision · `01JVM...`"

That's it. No wall of JSON, no repeating your words back at you.

## Backdating

If you're capturing something that happened earlier, tell the agent:

> "Note that this morning I decided to drop the caching layer."

The agent sets `created_at` to the time you specified, not now.
